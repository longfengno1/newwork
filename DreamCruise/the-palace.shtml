The Palace

Imagine your dream vacation, multiply it by 10, and this journey will take you beyond even that! Each day you can choose from a whopping 35 restaurants and bars serving up the finest Asian and international cuisines. One evening take in a Las Vegas-calibre cabaret show and the next pull an all-nighter in Asia's iocnic Zouk beach club at sea. Dozens of active sports include rock-climbing, waterslides and zip line, or you can experience the thrill of virtual reality in high-speed driving simulators. To relax and recharge for more non-stop fun, you can visit a Western, Asian or medical spa. There's such a wide array of activities to entertain your family and friends, this is a vacation they'll never forget.

Consists of the largest island in Thailand with 32 small islands, this is the perfect spot for sea, sun and sand. It is a paradise for water sports loverss and let's get your swimsuit ready. Visit the bustling street markets to experience the energetic soul of Phuket and check out the Old Town with over 100 years old Sino-colonial mansions to uncover the historical side of Phuket.
